Club Overview

Survivor Texas is a student-run organization at the University of Texas that simulates the CBS show Survivor. Over the past 5 seasons, we've grown from a small group of enthusiasts to a thriving community of over 100 members. Below is a quick glance at our seasons:

Season 1

Our inaugural season with 12 contestants and a small crew. A low-budget but unforgettable experience.

Season 2: Wild West

Expanded to 21 crew members and 12 contestants. Featured a Wild West theme with two tribes: Tumbleweeds and Dustdevils.

Club History

2021: Founding

Survivor Texas was founded by Cooper Wilk, Joyce, and Jonathan during their sophomore year at UT.

2022: Season 1

Our first season was a small-scale production with 12 contestants and a tight-knit crew.

2023: Season 2

Expanded to a larger crew and introduced themed gameplay with the Wild West season.

2024: Season 3

Currently in production with a growing crew and more ambitious plans.
